Picking The Raid 2 over The Raid Redemption (The original) is a bit controversial sure, but I stand by it. People that prefer the first film say that they enjoy the constant incredible action with minimal plot. I can see why they say this and critisize the sequel for its length and conveluted story. Sure. I understand. They will also say the reason all the plot isn't needed is because the reason you watch these films is for the action and fighting. I 100% agree. I enjoyed the first film very very much and it's definitely a close call, but the reason The Raid 2 is on my list is because even though they are broken up with a decent but hard to follow, the action scenes are better. In fact I'd say The Raid 2 has the best fight scene of all time, followed by a number of contenders for the best fight scene of all time. There is also a lot more variety. For example while the first film is set just in an apartment complex, The Raid 2 takes place firstly in a prizon, and then all around a city. There is even an amazing car chase. In my opinion this is the greatest action film of all time and (Welsh) director Gareth Evans is a genius. Here is a list of all the amazing action sequences in the film in the order they happen.

  1. The prison bathroom stall fight
  2. The prison riot
  3. The '$5000 short' scene
  4. The nightclub fight
  5. Hammer Girl and Bat guy VS the world
  6. Car chase
  7. Warehouse fight
  8. Hammer Girl and Bat guy VS Rama
  9. Kitchen Fight (The best fight scene ever to hit our screens)

I'm being very serious when I say every one of this sequences are fantastic. The Kitchen fight is about 10 minutes long and is a spectacle in choreography and violence. It's a joy to watch.
