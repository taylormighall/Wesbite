Borat is undisputedly Sacha Baron Cohen's magnum opus. In my opinion Borat is his greatest character and this is his greatest movie. The mockumentary style with mostly unscripted vignettes is a genius way to present this story of a man from (a fictional) Kazakhstan going to the US of A to at first make a documentary, and then to find Pamela Anderson. Instead of writing an essay like I have about the other films, I'll instead list some scenes and quotes that make this film so great.


  • "He is my neighbor Nursultan Tuliagby. He is pain in my assholes. I get a window from a glass, he must get a window from a glass. I get a step, he must get a step. I get a clock radio, he cannot afford. Great success!"

  • The naked fight

  • "May George Bush drink the blood of every man, woman, and child in Iraq!"

  • Borat singing the Kazakhstan national anthem at the start of a Rodeo

  • "I will forgive Pamela, and I will go to California with my friend Mr. Jesus, AND TOGETHER WE WILL TAKE HER!"

  • The deleted 'Cheese' scene

  • "Gypsy! Give me your tears! If you will not give them to me, I will take them from you!"

  • Borat accepting Jesus

  • "Even though my anus was broken, I knew the rest of our journey would be great success."
